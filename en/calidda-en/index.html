Cálidda - Sustainability report video

Illustrations and design for Cálidda.

2021

Project made in collaboration with Sabor Audiovisual

Design: María Teresa Romero

Illustration: María Teresa Romero, Paola Yataco.

Animation: Paola Yataco

You can watch the final video on Calidda's LinkedIn.

The client

Calidda is a natural gas company responsible for the implementation and distribution of natural gas in Lima. The company has an extensive social responsibility program that includes multiple areas, from climate change to community building.

To share the achievements and figures of this past year, they decided to produce an animated video.

The project

The objective of this project was to communicate the sustainability plan's most important milestones to Calidda's clients and stakeholders. There were some specific requirements:

  • The video had to be informative, playful and entertaining.
  • The video had to be coherent with Calidda's identity, both in visual terms and structure and tone.
  • It was of utmost importance to emphasize the core values specified in Calidda's sustainability pillars: Environment Protection, Urban Improvement and Connection Building.

The Process

I was in charge of developing a distinctive graphic identity for the video and illustrating 11 of the 16 final screens.

The client wanted a whiteboard type video. I took the main elements of this style (white background, thick lines, hand-written or script type) and adapted it to fit Calidda's brand. I used the brand colour, a vibrant cyan, to highlight characters and elements representative of the brand. I also included four accent colours (blue, green, orange and light yellow),

One of the main challenges of this project was communicating technical information, such as the natural gas network map, in a way that wouldn't clash with the visual style and tone of the video..

The final video is on Calidda's LinkedIn profile. You can find it here.
