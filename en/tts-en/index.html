TaxTech - Time Tax Support video

Design and illustrations for TaxTech, a software company.

2021

Project made in collaboration with Sabor Audiovisual

Design e Illustration: María Teresa Romero.

Animation: Paola Yataco.

You can watch the final video on  TTS's website

The Client

TaxTech Peru is a software solutions company specializing in tax management.

The project

For the website of one of its products, Time Tax Support, TaxTech required a simple explanatory video with a modern and lively style, which would explain the features and benefits of using the product.

The Process

For the visual style, I created a palette utilizing colour variations of the brand and product palette. I used blue, light blue and indigo as the primary colours for the video, complemented with yellow and orange accents to create a striking graphic presence for the product.

The visual style had to mesh seamlessly with the already existing assets and interface for the software. The character illustration was stylized and compatible with previous explainer videos the client has commissioned for their other products.


I also had to re-create all UI interfaces seen in the video, as the client didn't have any vector files ready for animation.
